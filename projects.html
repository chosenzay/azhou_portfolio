Diagram Design

Yeezy Shoes Real VS Fake

Adidas Yeezy 750 Boost is one of the most popular sneakers in 2015. Because of its limit number, the market price of this sneaker is $2500 and the price is still increasing. However, there’s a lot of fake Yeezys was sneaking into the market and the buyers will be fooled by some greedy sellers. So in this diagram, I made a comparison between the real yeezy and the fake one, and I also pointed out some important details with the red lines( so far there are three different levels of the fake yeezy, the one I chose was the second best quality fake Yeezy)
